Total Library Attendance Throughout the Years

Public libraries are a critical element of free education and community. Technology has made information more accessible, influencing library attendence.

Library attendance has gradually declined from 2013-2020 and gradually dropped due to Covid-19. As the pandemic has ended, more people are returning to Chicago public libraries, but in far fewer numbers.
