Content Plan

I am still unsure of what I want to do in the future - all I know for certain is that I would like to someday write a book. I am currently working at The Daily Northwestern as an advertising representative and production assistant, and I am enjoying the business side of Journalism. However, I also very much enjoy video editing and creative writing. I will try to demonstrate all of my interests by utilizing spreadsheets and showing various projects of mine.

As for newsrooms and positions I am targeting, I am interested in working for The Daily Herald. I have grown up with their newspaper and would love to work in my hometown. I would like to either edit videos or solicit advertisers. Throughout my professional career, I hope to tweak my current novels and try to get one published.

This website will show a variety of skills that I hope to use in my future job. I have included three video projects of mine - a profile, an event and a music video I made with my friend. I used Adobe Premiere Pro and VLLO for these. I have also included some of my creative works, which consist of poems, short stories and a novel. As a bookworm, I have also included a series of data visualizations about Chicago Public Libraries and their shrinking attendance numbers.
