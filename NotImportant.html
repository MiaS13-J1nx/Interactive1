The Once-ler is a character from Dr. Seuss's book "The Lorax." He is the narrator and the one responsible for the environmental destruction in the story.

The Once-ler cut down Truffula Trees to produce Thneeds, which led to the depletion of natural resources and environmental degradation.

The Once-ler's story serves as a cautionary tale about the consequences of environmental exploitation and the importance of sustainability.
